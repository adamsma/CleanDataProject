Codebook for "summarized_data.txt"
================

1) Subject_ID - identification number for the test subject (1-30)

2) Activity - the activity performed by the test subject 
(laying, sitting, standing, walking, walking downstairs, walking upstairs)

3-81) The remaining 79 variables are the mean values of the data measurement listed. 
These are the mean values of mean and std measurements from UCI Human Activity Recognition Using Smartphones Data Set. Each
value is therefore either a mean of a mean value or a mean of a std value. Moreover, that is to say "tBodyAcc-mean()-X" is 
the mean of all "tBodyAcc-mean()-X" measurements from the original data set for the subject and activity listed in columns 
1 and 2. Likewise, "tBodyAcc-std()-X" is the mean of all "tBodyAcc-std()-X" measurements from the original data set for the 
subject and activity listed in columns 1 and 2. Additional information for the original measurements can be found in the file 
"feature_info.txt" included with the original UCI data set. The complete list of mean values included in the tidy data set
